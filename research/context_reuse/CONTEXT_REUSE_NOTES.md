# Context reuse notes

Observation from ContextPilot-style systems:

- context reuse depends on structural stability
- reordered regions destroy reuse locality
- deterministic segmentation may improve cache reuse
- repeated regions are not necessarily semantic regions
- stable boundaries matter for retrieval and caching

Potential long-term relevance for GLYPH:

    deterministic structural segmentation

Not part of v0.2 roadmap.
