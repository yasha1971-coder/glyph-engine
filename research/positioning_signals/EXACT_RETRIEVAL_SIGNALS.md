# Exact retrieval signals

Status: research notes, not product claims.

## Observed industry direction

Approximate retrieval systems increasingly need verification stages.

Relevant pattern:
- adaptive / multi-stage retrieval
- verification after candidate generation
- exact confirmation after approximate search

## GLYPH hypothesis

GLYPH may serve as a deterministic exact-retrieval layer alongside approximate systems.

Not a replacement for semantic retrieval.

Core idea:

    approximate systems find candidates
    deterministic systems verify exact presence

## Current rule

Do not claim "solves vector hallucinations".

Use:

    deterministic verification layer
